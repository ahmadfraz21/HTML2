Most mine claim owners think surface cleanup is a cost. We make it income.

Across the American West, thousands of old mine claims sit with piles of waste rock and tailings on the surface. For most owners those piles are a liability — an environmental risk and a cleanup headache. We see them differently.

Those surface piles contain residual valuable material. We pay you for them — and we clean them up. It's that simple.

What Makes Our Process Different
▶

No underground work — we only remove surface waste, never disturb what's below
▶

Site restoration included — each cleared section is backfilled, remediated, and revegetated
▶

You retain your rights — land title and all underground mineral reserves remain 100% yours
▶

Environmental liability eliminated — old mine waste carries real legal and cleanup risk; we remove it
▶

Nonprofit mission — we're a registered nonprofit dedicated to cleaning up the American West
